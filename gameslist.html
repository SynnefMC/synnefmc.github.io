Games you should try out

Celeste - challenging 2D platforming, amazing story

Hollow Knight - Metroidvania game with great platforming and hard fights
Spin Rhythm XD - innovating rhythm game currently in early access
Undertale - JRPG with a heavy focus on letting the player decide the fate of their playthrough
A Hat in Time - excessively cute 3D collect-a-thon platformer, consider buying the DLCs if you enjoy it, base game is quite short
Furi - boss fighting game with decent difficulty if you like a challenge
Just Shapes & Beats - rhythm bullet hell, epilepsy warning
This list may be updated in the future.
